WHEREAS the Consultant is duly licensed to provide immigration consultancy services in the United Arab Emirates; and

WHEREAS the Client wishes to engage the Consultant to provide end-to-end immigration consultancy services for the purpose of applying for a visa;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants set out herein, the parties agree as follows:

1 Scope of Services

The Consultant agrees to provide the following end-to-end immigration consultancy services ("Services") to the Client:

  • Onboarding & Assessment: Initial consultation, eligibility assessment, and personalised immigration pathway advice.
  • Documentation: Preparation, review, and compilation of all required documents including CV, cover letters, educational assessments, and supporting evidence.
  • Application Submission: Preparation and submission of the visa/work permit application to the relevant immigration authority on behalf of the Client.
  • Liaison & Follow-up: Communication with immigration authorities, embassies, and third-party agencies regarding the application status.
  • Biometric & Appointment Coordination: Scheduling and coordination of biometric appointments and embassy/consulate visits.
  • Pre-Departure Support: Guidance on travel, settlement, and arrival requirements in the destination country.
  • Air Ticket Arrangement: Coordination of international air travel (UAE to destination) as a disbursement service.

3 Client Obligations

The Client agrees to:

  1. Provide all required documents, information, and disclosures truthfully and in a timely manner.
  2. Disclose any previous visa refusals, criminal records, or immigration history honestly and completely.
  3. Attend all scheduled appointments (biometric, embassy, medical) promptly.
  4. Make all milestone payments on their respective due dates.
  5. Notify the Consultant immediately of any changes in personal circumstances (employment, address, marital status) that may affect the application.
  6. Not engage any other immigration consultant for the same application without prior written consent.

4 Consultant Obligations

The Consultant agrees to:

  1. Provide Services with reasonable skill, care, and diligence in accordance with UAE professional standards.
  2. Keep the Client's personal data strictly confidential and use it solely for immigration purposes.
  3. Provide regular progress updates throughout the application process.
  4. Submit all government fees and disbursements at exact cost, evidenced by third-party receipts.
  5. Maintain all required licences and regulatory registrations throughout the engagement.

5 No Guarantee of Outcome

Important: The Consultant does not guarantee a successful visa or immigration outcome. Immigration decisions rest solely with the relevant government authorities. The Consultant's obligation is to provide competent professional services — not to guarantee approval.

The Client acknowledges that visa refusal does not entitle the Client to a refund of the consultancy fee (Line 1), as the professional services will have been rendered. Government disbursements (Lines 2–4) will be refunded only to the extent recoverable from third parties.

6 Refund & Cancellation Policy

  • Before Application Submission: 50% of the consultancy fee (AED 4,000) is refundable if the Client cancels after onboarding but before submission.
  • After Application Submission: The consultancy fee (AED 8,000) is non-refundable.
  • Disbursements: Refundable only if not yet paid to the relevant government authority or airline.
  • Visa Refusal: No refund of consultancy fee. Disbursements refunded to the extent recovered from authorities.

7 Confidentiality & Data Protection

Both parties agree to keep all information exchanged under this Agreement strictly confidential. The Consultant shall process the Client's personal data in compliance with applicable UAE data protection laws and shall not disclose it to any third party except as required for the provision of Services (e.g., submission to immigration authorities, embassies).

8 Termination

  • By Client: The Client may terminate this Agreement by providing 14 days' written notice. Refund provisions in Clause 6 apply.
  • By Consultant: The Consultant may terminate immediately if the Client provides false information, fails to pay, or engages in conduct that jeopardises the application or the Consultant's professional standing.
  • Upon termination, all original documents provided by the Client shall be returned within 7 business days.

9 Governing Law & Dispute Resolution

This Agreement is governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the United Arab Emirates. Any dispute arising out of or in connection with this Agreement shall first be referred to mediation. If unresolved within 30 days, disputes shall be submitted to the courts of Dubai, UAE.

10 Entire Agreement

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior representations, discussions, and arrangements. Amendments must be in writing and signed by both parties.
